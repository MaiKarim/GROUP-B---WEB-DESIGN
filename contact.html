Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our agency and services.

How do I apply for player representation?

Submit your player profile through our contact form or send an email to talent@footballagency.sl with your playing history, videos, and contact information. Our scouting team reviews all submissions.

What are your agency fees?

We operate on a commission basis only after successfully securing a contract for a player. There are no upfront fees for representation. Our standard commission is 10% of the player's contract value.

Do you represent female footballers?

Yes, we represent both male and female footballers. Our agency is committed to developing all football talent in Sierra Leone, regardless of gender.
