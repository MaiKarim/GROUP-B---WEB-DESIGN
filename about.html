About Football Agency Sierra Leone

Discover our mission, vision, and the team dedicated to developing Sierra Leone's football talent.

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Football Agency Sierra Leone emerged from a vision to create professional pathways for the abundant yet untapped football talent in Sierra Leone. Our founders, former professional footballers and sports management experts, recognized the need for a structured, professional agency to bridge the gap between local talent and international opportunities.

Over the years, we have grown to become Sierra Leone's leading football agency, representing over 50 professional footballers and facilitating transfers to clubs in Europe, Asia, and other African nations.

Our agency operates with transparency, professionalism, and a deep commitment to the personal and professional development of every player we represent.

Our Mission

To identify, develop, and promote Sierra Leonean football talent through professional representation, career management, and international exposure, creating sustainable career pathways in global football.

Our Vision

To become the premier football agency in West Africa, recognized globally for excellence in player representation and for transforming Sierra Leone into a powerhouse of football talent development.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Football Agency Sierra Leone.

Integrity

We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical practices in all our dealings with players, clubs, and partners.

Player-Centric

Our players' interests, welfare, and career development are at the heart of every decision we make.

Development

We invest in continuous player development, both on and off the pitch, to ensure long-term success.
